Our Story

This year, I lost my beautiful and brilliant sister unexpectedly. In the days that followed, family members and I found ourselves in search of appropriate memorial attire. Heavy with grief, I scrolled clothing websites and walked through stores filled with holiday lights and cheerful music, thinking—there has to be a better way. Like most women, I don't have time to just shop; I shop by need, by life's moments—I shop By Occasion. And like many, I often waste valuable time searching dozens of sites before finding just the right outfit. That's why I created By Occasion: to make finding the perfect outfit easier, so you can focus on what truly matters—showing up as your most confident self for life's important moments.
